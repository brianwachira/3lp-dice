How to play
  • Roll the Dice. Any number other than 1 will be added to your score.
  • If you get 1 all your score will be reduced to 0
  • Click hold to prevent your current score from reducing to 0 . It holds your score
  • Any score not hold will be reduced to 0
  • 1st person to reach 100 wins
